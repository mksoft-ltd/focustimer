# Privacy Policy for Focus Timer

**Last Updated: December 1, 2024**

## Introduction

Welcome to Focus Timer ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Focus Timer mobile application (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Focus Timer stores the following data locally on your device:

- **Task Information**: Task titles, descriptions, start times, durations, and completion status
- **Focus Reasons**: Personal reasons you add for staying focused on tasks
- **App Preferences**: Your settings and preferences within the App
- **Subscription Status**: Whether you have an active subscription or trial

This data is stored locally using secure on-device storage and is **not transmitted to our servers**.

### Information Collected Automatically

We do **not** collect:
- Personal identification information
- Location data
- Device identifiers for tracking purposes
- Usage analytics or behavioral data

### Calendar Access

If you choose to import tasks from your device calendar, the App will request permission to read your calendar data. This data is:
- Only accessed when you explicitly choose to import events
- Processed entirely on your device
- Never transmitted to external servers
- Never stored beyond the import process

### In-App Purchases

When you make a purchase through the App:
- All payment processing is handled securely by Google Play
- We do not collect or store your payment information
- We only receive confirmation of your subscription status from Google Play

## How We Use Your Information

The information stored locally on your device is used to:

- Display your scheduled tasks and focus sessions
- Send local notifications to remind you of upcoming tasks
- Track your subscription status to provide appropriate features
- Maintain your app preferences and settings

## Data Storage and Security

- All your data is stored locally on your device
- We do not operate servers that store your personal data
- Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features
- If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data will be deleted

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

### Google Play Billing
- Used for processing in-app purchases and subscriptions
- Subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### Local Notifications
- Used to send task reminders on your device
- No data is transmitted externally

## Children's Privacy

Focus Timer is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: View all your data within the App
- **Delete**: Remove any or all tasks and data through the App's interface
- **Uninstall**: Remove the App and all associated data from your device

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy within the App
- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

## Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device for as long as you have the App installed. You can delete individual tasks at any time through the App. Uninstalling the App will remove all stored data.

## Do Not Track

The App does not track users across third-party websites or services, and therefore does not respond to Do Not Track signals.
